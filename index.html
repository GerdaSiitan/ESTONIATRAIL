Your Guide to Estonian Wilderness!

Welcome to the ultimate hiking companion for exploring the stunning landscapes of Estonia!

Explore Estonia's Natural Beauty with Our Hike App

Estonia is a hidden gem for outdoor enthusiasts, with its pristine forests, coastal paths, bogs, and captivating wilderness. Our app helps you find the perfect hike for your interests and skill level.

Detailed Trail Information

Once you've selected a hike, our app provides you with all the information you need to make the most of your experience. Explore interactive maps, detailed trail descriptions, elevation profiles, and trailhead directions. You'll have everything at your fingertips to plan a successful outing.
